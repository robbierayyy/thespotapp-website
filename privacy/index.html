Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Summary: TheSpot collects minimal data necessary to provide personalized place recommendations. We don't sell your data to third parties.

1. Information We Collect

When you use TheSpot, we collect:

  • Account Information: Email address and name when you create an account
  • Location Data: Your location to provide nearby recommendations (only when you grant permission)
  • Usage Data: Your searches, saved places, and preferences to personalize recommendations
  • Device Information: Device type and operating system for app optimization

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide personalized place recommendations based on your mood and preferences
  • Save your favorite places and plans
  • Improve our recommendation algorithms
  • Send notifications about events and saved plans (if enabled)
  • Process subscription payments through Apple

3. Third-Party Services

TheSpot uses the following third-party services:

  • Supabase: Database and authentication services
  • Google Places API: Place information and photos
  • Yelp Fusion API: Business ratings and reviews
  • Ticketmaster: Event information
  • RevenueCat: Subscription management
  • Apple: Payment processing and Sign in with Apple
  • Google: Sign in with Google

Each service has its own privacy policy governing how they handle your data.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data:

  • With service providers necessary to operate the app
  • When required by law
  • With your consent (e.g., sharing lists publicly)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View your personal data in the app
  • Delete: Delete your account and all associated data through Settings
  • Opt-out: Disable notifications and location services
  • Export: Request a copy of your data by contacting us

7. Children's Privacy

TheSpot is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
