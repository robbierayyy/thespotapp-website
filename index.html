Frequently Asked Questions

How does TheSpot work?

TheSpot uses AI to understand your mood and preferences, then curates local spots that match your vibe. Just tell us how you're feeling—looking for a cozy coffee spot, a lively bar, or a quiet place to focus—and we'll do the rest.

Is TheSpot free to use?

TheSpot offers a free tier with limited daily searches. Upgrade to Premium for unlimited vibe searches, exclusive mood filters, and full access to all recommendations.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime through your iPhone's Settings, then Apple ID, then Subscriptions, then TheSpot. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period.

What cities does TheSpot support?

TheSpot works anywhere with Google Places coverage. We're currently optimized for major US cities including San Diego, Los Angeles, New York, Chicago, Austin, and more.

How do I delete my account?

To delete your account and all associated data, go to Settings then Account then Delete Account within the app, or email us at support@thespotapp.xyz.
